Welcome to The Muse

A Cure for Writer's Block

Welcome to The Muse! Here you will be given a prompt of a quote or picture where you will have whatever amount of time you wish in order to write a stream of consciousness! You'll have 5 minutes to write! Happy Writing!

Look over your stream and see if any words or phrases stick out to you. Log them into the list on the left! Let them sit there for a bit and see if any prose pops out. Then, write it down on the text box on the right!
